Our Mission

At AI Code Detector, we're transforming how developers work with AI-generated code. Our advanced detection algorithms help identify potential issues, security vulnerabilities, and optimization opportunities in your codebase.

Founded in 2023, we've grown from a small research project to an industry-leading platform trusted by thousands of developers worldwide. Our team of AI experts and software engineers are dedicated to making code analysis smarter, faster, and more accessible.

We believe that AI should enhance human creativity, not replace it. That's why we've built tools that help developers understand and improve AI-generated code while maintaining full control over their projects.
